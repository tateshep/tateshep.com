My Story

Let's get to know each other

Hello

My name is Tate Shepherd. I am a web designer/developer based in Bend, Oregon. About 2 years ago I built a personal Squarespace photography portfolio and blog. This project intrigued me with the process of organizing content and displaying it in a beautiful way. Soon after starting my blog, a desire for a deeper understanding of how the web works grew within me. The logical path following my toying around with a Content Management System such as Squarespace, was to learn the building block languages of a website. I took some introductory web design classes at Udemy.com where I learned the web languages; HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, and more.

Since my first Squarespace website, I have personally owned and built 5 different websites using a variety of techniques. I have built an Ecommerce store with Shopify, a blog with both Squarespace and Wordpress, and coded websites ground up with nothing more the HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I particularly enjoy using Bootstrap or a similar CSS library to set the foundation of my sites, and then further customize how I see fit. Additionally I have helped many friends on small business sites. I really enjoy seeing small businesses get off the ground, and take great pleasure in being a part of that process.

In parallel to my interests in web developement, I have become quite passionate about Blockchain technology. The potential for good applications with transparent immutable decentralized ledger technology is undeniable. Staying up to date with this technology requires a lot of work. I am constantly researching and discovering new projects that are very exciting.

When I am not learning more about technology, I love enjoying the outdoors surrounding Bend. My main activity is rock climbing. But I also enjoy fly-fishing, and photography. I am fortunate to be living in a beautiful place, and try to take advantage of this as often as possible!
